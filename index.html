Popular Spots

Burnham Park

A scenic park with boating, skating, and picnic areas.

Open daily: 6 AM - 9 PM.

Entry Fee: Free

Nearby: Night Market, Cafes

Accessibility: Wheelchair Accessible

BenCab Museum

A cultural and art museum featuring works by the National Artist Benedicto Cabrera.

Working Hour:

Nearby: Tam-awan Village

Entry Fee: ₱150 (Senior/PWD with valid ID)

Other Details:

Nearby: Tam-awan Village

Accessibility: Not wheelchair accessible

Working Hours

Tuesdays to Sundays: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (last entry at 5:30 PM)
Closed: Mondays, Christmas Day, and New Year’s Day

Other Details

Admission to the Museum:

Php 200 – General admission
Php 150 – Senior/PWD with valid ID
Php 120 – Student with valid Philippine school-issued ID
Free – Child no taller than 42″
Special rates for student & organized groups with a minimum of 30 guests

EcoTrail Tour:

Php 200 – Regular fee
Php 150 – Senior/PWD with valid ID
Php 120 – Student with valid Philippine school-issued ID
Php 150 – Guide fee per group*

*An Eco-trail Guide will be assigned to accompany children (aged 6 to 12) in groups of 10 and other hikers (aged 13 onwards) in groups of 15.

Visitor Guidelines:

  • Guided tours for the museum & Eco-trail are available upon inquiry.
  • Photography: No flash, drones, tripods, or selfie sticks allowed inside.
  • Food & beverages allowed only in Café Sabel.
  • Large bags must be left at the reception.
  • Strict rules for personal conduct, including respecting other visitors and avoiding disruptive behavior.

Mines View Park

A viewpoint offering panoramic views of Benguet’s mining towns and mountains. Open daily: 8 AM - 8 PM.

Entry Fee: Free

Nearby: Good Shepherd

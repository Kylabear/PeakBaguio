Burnham Park

A haven for relaxation and fun activities in the heart of Baguio City.

Burnham Park

History: Established in 1925 by American architect Daniel Burnham, this park is one of the oldest and most iconic recreational spaces in Baguio City. Known for its scenic beauty, Burnham Park offers paddle boating, biking, and flower gardens that attract thousands of visitors each year.

Accessibility

Wheelchair accessible pathways, nearby public restrooms, and convenient transportation hubs make Burnham Park accessible to everyone.

Emergency Contacts

PNP Burnham Station: +63 912 345 6789
Medical Assistance: +63 987 654 3210

Mines View Park

A breathtaking panoramic view of the mining town of Itogon.

Mines View Park

History: Mines View Park was established to offer a stunning view of the old mining town of Itogon. It became a popular tourist destination for those wanting to experience the beauty of Baguio’s mountains and learn about the area’s mining history.

Accessibility

Mines View Park is easily accessible by private vehicles or jeepneys from the city center. Wheelchair accessible pathways are available for visitors with disabilities.

Emergency Contacts

Medical Assistance: +63 987 654 3210

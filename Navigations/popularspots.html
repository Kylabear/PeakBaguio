Burnham Park

A haven for relaxation and fun activities in the heart of Baguio City.

(4.5 based on user reviews)

Do's

  • Respect the park's cleanliness.
  • Follow park regulations.
  • Enjoy paddle boating and biking.

Don'ts

  • No littering or vandalism.
  • Avoid feeding animals.

Parking Availability

Available parking spaces near Burnham Park Plaza.

Recommended Transportation

Best explored on foot or by bike. Jeepney terminals are nearby for public transport.

Accessibility

Wheelchair-friendly ramps and pathways are available throughout the park.

Emergency Contacts

PNP: +63 912 345 6789
Medical Assistance: +63 987 654 3210

Location

Jeepney Terminal: Located at Harisson Road near Burnham Park.
